## Go package

### testify

[stretchr/testify](https://github.com/stretchr/testify) go的测试包，不用写`if err!=nil`进行测试

### jwt-go

[dgrijalva/jwt-go](https://github.com/dgrijalva/jwt-go) 认证的包

### PASETO

[o1egl/paseto](https://github.com/o1egl/paseto) 基于token的认证库，比JWT更安全
